Burrito

Recipe Description

The burrito consists of a tortilla and various contents of different kinds. It usually sports a base like rice, protein like chicken, and veggies. Sauces optional

Ingredients

  • Tortilla
  • Cilantro Lime Rice
  • Black Beans
  • Chicken
  • Pico De Gallo
  • Corn
  • Sour Cream
  • Lettuce

Steps

  1. Cook chicken and cut into bite size pieces
  2. Cook rice, add lime zest and a bit of lime juice
  3. Dice Tomatoes, Onions, and Serrano Pepper
    • Combine in bowl and add lime juice
  4. Open can of beans and put aside
  5. Chop lettuce to bite size pieces
  6. Heat up tortilla
  7. Add rice, beans, chicken, pico de gallo, corn, lettuce, and sour cream to tortilla
  8. Roll up the tortilla
