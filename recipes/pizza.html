Pizza

Recipe Description

One of the greatest Mod pizzas there ever were! Slightly modified to taste, and delicious when enjoyed with a special someone

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough
  • Marinara sauce
  • Mozarella cheese
  • Asiago cheese
  • Roasted red peppers
  • Roasted garlic
  • Mushrooms
  • Salami
  • Pesto

Steps

  1. Knead pizza dough
  2. Add marinara sauce to dough
  3. Add toppings throughout
    • Salami on one side only
  4. Bake pizza
  5. Add pesto sauce while pizza is hot
