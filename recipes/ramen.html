Ramen

Recipe Description

There is beauty in simplicity and struggle meals. The instant ramen is one of them. This recipe pioneered by my aunt. Which adds Mexican flare to it as well as umami flavors thanks to dark sauces.

Ingredients

  • Instant Ramen (shrimp recommended)
  • Maggi Sauce
  • English (worcestershire) Sauce
  • Hot sauce (Valentina)
  • Lime

Steps

  1. Cut lime in half
  2. Cook ramen according to instructions
  3. Once Ramen is cooked, add sauces
    • 5 part English sauce
    • 2 part Maggi sauce
    • 1 part Hot sauce
  4. Add lime juice
    • If it's a big lime, just half. If it's a small lime, the whole thing
